Ultimate Spirit analysis

I mentioned that here are so many poeple who don't know how to analyse the spirit sheets on tournaments. So this software will do that for you. All you have to do is creating a tournament, add all Teams, and fill out the spirit sheet in the software.

You will get a live time ranking and a full usable analysis you can send to the teams after the tournament.
